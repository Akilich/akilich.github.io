Print and Digital Design

Mobile App Tutorial
Project Date 2018
Role Menu Design, Print Design
Employer Capriotti's Sandwhich Shop
The Why:

The company wanted to redesign their outdated corporate and franchise menus to reflect a more modern look. In addition, the company aslo wanted to add new menu items and become menu compliant. Once a menu design is established, the mobile app and online ordering platform will be redesigned to reflect the modern changes.

The Solve:

After several rounds of designs, the company agreed to roll out some example menus displayed above. The menus were then printed and updated in all Corporate and Franchise shops. Digital Menus were also updated and digital files sent to shops who has digital menus. Shortly after, the mobile app and online ordering website were updated to reflect the modern look and menu changes.

Tools Photoshop, Illustrator, Sketch, InDesign
